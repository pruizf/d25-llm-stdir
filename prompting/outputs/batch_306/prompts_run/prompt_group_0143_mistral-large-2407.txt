- Category 0

  - Definition: Aside (character addresses audience or is alone).
- Category 3
  - Name: delivery
  - Definition: Delivery manner, e.g. regarding voice or vocal expression of emotion. Also used when the character sings.   - Name: entrance

- Category 5
  - Name: exit

- Category 6
  - Name: interaction

- Category 7
  - Name: movement

- Category 8
  - Name: music
